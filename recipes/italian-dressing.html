Italian Dressing

Description

Tasty oil and red wine vinegar based italian dressing goes great with salads, vegatables and fresh greens

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 4 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/8 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/16 teaspoon celery seeds

Steps

  1. Place everything in a blender and blend for about 30 seconds (small of flecks of Parmesan will remain)
  2. Pour into a jar or dressing dispenser and refrigerate for at least 4 hours before using
  3. Store in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks
