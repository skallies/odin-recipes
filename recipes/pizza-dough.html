Pizza Dough

Description

Great tasting pizza dough for a medium crust. Not too thick and bread-like but not paper thin either.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • Extra oil for greasing bowl
  • Extra flour for rolling dough

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt
  2. Place warm water into a separate large mixing bowl
  3. Add yeast and sugar to water and stir gently until yeast starts to expand
  4. Add oil to water and yeast mixture
  5. Gradually mix flour and salt into the water mixture
  6. Form dough into a ball and powder with flour
  7. Coat large bowl very lightly with olive oil
  8. Place dough into oiled bowl and cover with a clean lint-free towel
  9. Allow dough to rise for 1-2 hours in a warm area
  10. If the room is chilly, put dough in an un-heated oven, covered
  11. Put hot water into a pot, cover and place in oven beside bowl of dough
  12. Roll out dough, cover with pizza sauce, Mozzarella cheese and preferred toppings.
